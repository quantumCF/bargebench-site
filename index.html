before you send anything

QIs this just a script grading itself?

No. Every flagged barge-in event gets a human mark in addition to the audio-timing measurement. The math is public and auditable. The judgment call on each event is made by a person, not the same system scoring itself.

QWhy pay for this when the harness is free?

You get independent judgment, a signed attestation a third party will trust, and the interruptions your own logs never captured. The open harness gives you the raw numbers to check our work or run yourself; the report gives you a verdict you can stand behind.

QWhat if my agent cuts callers off too eagerly instead?

Out of scope. This report scores the agent talking over the caller during barge-in, not the opposite failure mode.

QWho should not buy this?

Skip this if you have not shipped to production yet, if you already run a barge-in eval in CI, or if you do not run real voice or phone calls.

QWhat if you find nothing?

Full refund, you pay nothing. You also keep a dated, signed attestation that your barge-in handling came back clean, which you can show a customer or your board.

QIs this a subscription I'll forget to cancel?

No. One time, per batch of up to 15 calls. No seats, nothing to forget to cancel. One re-scored, re-attested retest is included within 60 days if you ship a fix.

QWhat actually happens to my recordings after?

Deleted within 14 days of delivery. Scored on our own US hardware, never a third-party API, never used to train any model. NDA or DPA available on request. Scoring reads timing, not words, so you can redact PHI first.

QWhat shows up on my card statement?

Your card statement will show the billing entity, named in the payment section below. It's BargeBench's parent company, nothing unfamiliar.
