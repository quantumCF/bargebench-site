before you send anything

Questions a skeptical buyer actually asks.

QIs this just a script grading itself?

No. Every flagged barge-in event gets a human mark in addition to the audio-timing measurement. The math is public and auditable. The judgment call on each event is made by a person, not the same system scoring itself.

QWhy pay $499 for something the open harness does for free?

If you're self-hosted with dual-channel audio, you can probably pull raw numbers yourself in about a day. What that does not get you: independent judgment, a signed attestation a third party will trust, or a catch on what your own logs never captured.

QWhat if my agent cuts callers off too eagerly instead?

Out of scope. This report scores the agent talking over the caller during barge-in, not the opposite failure mode.

QI'm on Retell, isn't this already covered by Retell Assure?

Retell shipped a native automated QA feature, Retell Assure, in January 2026, which monitors calls for interruptions and other issues inside the platform. If you're already relying on that, you may get less incremental value from an outside report than someone without built-in monitoring. Worth weighing before you buy.

QIs this a subscription I'll forget to cancel?

No. $499, flat, one time, per batch of up to 15 calls. No seats. One re-scored, re-attested retest is included within 60 days if you ship a fix.

QWhat actually happens to my recordings after?

Deleted within 14 days of delivery. Scored on our own hardware in the US, never a third-party API, never used to train any model.
